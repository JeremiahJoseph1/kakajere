Karibu GBTC. Hii ni ngazi ya cheti ya Shule ya Huduma na inasomwa kwa muda wa miezi minne. Ngazi hii ina masomo nane. Notsi za haya masomo yapo hapa kwenye menyu ya 'Downloads'. Pia unaweza kujifunza masomo haya kwa njia ya video au sauti ambazo pia zipo hapa ndani kwenye menyu za 'Videos' na 'Audios'. Matokeo ya mitihani yataonekana kwenye menyu ya 'Academics' pindi yatakapotolewa na mhadhiri wetu Askofu E. S. Ekong. Kuona vyote vya GBTC vilivyomo kwenye tovuti hii angalia kwenye menyu ya 'Snapshots'. Wewe kama mwanafunzi tafadhali hakikisha picha yako ya paspoti na jina lako kwenye menyu ya 'Profiles' na ikiwa haviko sawa basi bonyeza kitufe hiki chini ya picha yako kisha uandike hapo jina lako kama unavyohitaji liandikwe katika rekodi zako katika chuo hiki. Tunakupenda. Welcome to GBTC. This is the certificate level of the School of Ministry and is studied over a four months period of time. This level has eight subjects. Notes for these lessons are here in the 'Downloads' menu. You can also learn these lessons through video or audio that are also available here in the 'Videos' and 'Audios' menus. The results of the exams will appear in the 'Academics' menu when they are released by our lecturer Bishop E. S. Ekong. To see all the GBTC content on this site look at the 'Snapshots' menu. You, as a student, please check your passport photo and your name in the 'Profiles' menu and if they are not correct, then press this button under your photo and then write your name there as you need it to be written in your records in this college. We love you. Good to go
