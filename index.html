Frequently asked questions

1. What are your opening hours?

So we know that drop off and pick up can be a military operation to fit in around you getting to and from work. Which is why most of our nurseries offer early starts and late finishes. They vary slightly between locations so ask on the day for your local nursery timings.

2. What activities do you provide?

We do loads! Eco-School, Boogie Mites, Intergenerational Play… to name just a few. Every day is packed full of engaging and educational activities to help your little one blossom. Make sure you find out more about these and other activities on your visit.

3. What feedback about my child’s day will I receive? In genereal.

Of course you want to know what your child is getting up to each day! That’s why every parent has access to Parent Zone which we use to send you photos and update you on your little one’s progress. The teams will tell you more about this on your visit, but it’ll definitely give you confidence that your little one’s being well cared for and having an amazing time.

4. How can I be involved in my child’s learning? Please tell me.

Parent Zone is your go to place to find out what your little one has been up to and how they’re getting on. But our teams are there any time to chat things through and to work together on learning and development. Find out more about this on your visit.

5. Do you take children into the local community or on outings?

Regular sessions at forest schools or trips to local nursing homes to spend time with the elderly residents is just an example. Schemes like these can have huge benefits on the children’s development so is something we encourage. Your local nursery can tell you much more about this, including the local communities we team up with.

6. What do your fees include? Please just tell me in summary.

Our fees are all inclusive so everything is provided. That’s nappies, wipes, creams etc. If you’re interested in which brands and types we use, ask at our open day.

7. Are all staff first aid trained? Just in case of an emergency

Keeping every child safe and well is our top priority. That’s why every single member of the team is fully trained in paediatric first aid meaning they are confident to act when needed. In fact, we’re proud to say we’re 100% rated with OSHA.

8. What happens if my little one becomes poorly or has an accident at nursery?

Kids get poorly… quite a lot! After all, it’s a given with little ones around. Whenever this happens, we’ll be in touch with you to discuss what we can do or if we need you to come and pick them up. We have a full policy which our local nurseries can share with you on the day.

9. Can I look at the menu? Is it inclusive of all kinds of diets?

Healthy living and wellbeing is so important to us. We have autumn/winter and spring/summer menus which are packed full of both nutritious but very scrummy meals! And naturally we support any dietary requirements. Chat to the team on your visit to get the full lowdown and to discuss any needs your little one may have.

10. What is the procedure when someone else collects my child?

The safety of your little one is top of the list. We have very strict procedures in place around who has access to the nursery and who can pick up etc. Ask at our open day to find out how this all works.
